Our Mission

Gifted KKnots is a handcrafted homegrown gifting brand specializing in personalized crochet creations, accessories, and thoughtful gift hampers. Each piece is uniquely made with love, care, and creativity to add a personal touch to every occasion. We’re also open to custom requests — whether it’s a bespoke crochet bouquet, a heartfelt note card, or other special additions—so your gift is as unique as the bond you’re celebrating.
